Data is the material that makes up the majority of our experiences today, especially with the stay home orders of COVID-19 in place. With these maps I seek to visualize the relationship between data as material and the resulting "culture of moments" described by Alexander Manu in his book "Transforming Organizations".

My contention is that today's culture of moments will result in what I have named The Opt-In Future, a future wherein augmentation and automation transform our lives, giving us the opportunity to focus on the things that matter most to us.

This is a work in progress and will be updated regularly.

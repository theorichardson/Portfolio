I am the designer at Xesto, a University of Toronto accelerated computer vision startup. This is the app and brand identity I designed for the company.

The app harnesses the power of the FaceID camera in iPhones X and above to help users scan and size their feet for online shoe shopping.

The icon consists of overlapping viewfinders that also form a crooked, playful X. The brand colours are bright, accessible and ownable. Xesto is available for download on the App Store for iPhones X and above.

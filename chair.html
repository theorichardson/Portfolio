These are generatively designed chairs created in collaboration with my MacBook and Autodesk's Fusion360 Generative Design software.

The ABS plastic chairs are optimized for strength and stiffness while minimizing material use and weight. All 3 chairs came from the same study with the same parameters.

Generative design will transform the design process through deep augmentation of human expression. Autodesk has a head start on this innovation.



Fusion360 Generative Design software is a signal of what's to come. Designers, engineers and normal users alike will embrace the human role of visionary while software produces more software for us. Implementation will be automated and human creativity will be augmented.
